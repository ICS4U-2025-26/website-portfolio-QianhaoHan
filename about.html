About Me

Get to know me a little better - my background & interests

Hello!

I'm Qianhao Han, a student at The Bishop Strachan School in Toronto (Class of 2027). I'm passionate about AI, robotics, and using technology to make a positive impact on the world.

CS & AI

I represented Team Canada at IOAI 2024 and 2025 (Silver in Practical, Bronze in Scientific) and IAIO 2026, where I won a Bronze Medal

Some recent highlights:

  • Research - Using deep learning for radiology report generation, published at IEEE BigData 2024 (Runner-Up Paper Award) and IEEE CBMS 2025
  • HiveGuard - A honeycomb-inspired concussion helmet with EEG feedback, accepted as a demo to ACM/IEEE SEC 2025 (2nd in Student Research Competition)
  • Tech2Gether - Co-founded a non-profit supporting financially disadvantaged robotics students, raising $15,000+ in sponsorships
  • OneAI - Founded a global AI education initiative with branches in 20+ schools across 5 countries
