Resources & Equity Information

Why This Matters

Robotics programs offer incredible learning opportunities in engineering, programming, teamwork, and problem-solving. However, systemic barriers prevent many students from participating:

  • Cost: Competition fees, parts, and travel can exceed $5,000-$15,000 per season
  • Access: Schools in lower-income areas often lack funding for extracurricular STEM programs
  • Awareness: Newcomer families may not know about available grants and sponsorships
  • Time: Students from working families may have less time for after-school activities

By planning budgets carefully and connecting teams with resources, we can help make STEM education more equitable across Canada.
